## Structural Analysis (Why This Script Works)

This script demonstrates several key techniques that make horror storytelling effective:

1. **Concrete numbers in the opening** (2019, population 23, youngest resident 67) -- establishes reality and credibility
2. **Countdown technique** (3:30, 3:31, 3:32, 3:33) -- builds unbearable tension through silence
3. **Sentence length reduction during tension** -- lines get shorter as fear increases
4. **The perpetrator IS the sympathetic character** -- the twist reframes everything
5. **Mundane final line from the ghost** ("Can I go inside now?") -- everyday words are more terrifying than screams
6. **Documentary gap technique** -- the official record (absent: cold) vs. the truth (teacher's punishment)
7. **Doubling at the end** ("used to be one crying, now there are two") -- implies the teacher's soul is now trapped too
8. **The teeth** -- a visceral, unexplainable physical detail that lingers in the mind
