Avoid identity drift:
- different person
- younger or older age band
- changed face shape
- changed hair silhouette
- changed body proportions
- different clothing silhouette
- extra people
- cropped head
- full-body framing when half-body is required
- unreadable text
- UI overlays
- phone screen UI
- watermark
- logo
- realistic photo style
- 3D render style
- heavy blur
