# Part 3 Pacing Guide — 감동 라디오 드라마 (Resolution)

## PURPOSE: Truth revealed. Emotional catharsis. Warm but bittersweet ending that lingers.

## TENSION TARGET: 6 → 7 → 8 → 5 (catharsis into lingering warmth)

## STRUCTURE (Target: 30-40 dialogue turns, ~3.5 minutes)

### Final Resolve (turns 1-8): Tension 6
- Protagonist makes a decision — to visit, to speak, to confront the truth
- Brief moment of courage or determination
- ★ CHARACTER ACTION: Taking concrete steps (getting on a bus, knocking on a door, opening a letter)
- The audience knows this is the final chance

### Truth Revealed (turns 9-20): Tension 7-8
- The emotional truth comes out — what was hidden, why it was hidden
- All foreshadowing from Parts 1-2 clicks into place
- ★ FORESHADOWING PAYOFF: All 3 elements connect to THIS moment
- Peak emotion: the listener should feel tears forming
- ★★★ THE EMOTIONAL CLIMAX must hit between turns 15-22 ★★★

### Catharsis (turns 21-30): Tension 7 → 5
- The breakthrough moment — forgiveness, understanding, or acceptance
- Characters finally say what they couldn't say before
- ★ One quiet, devastatingly simple line (할머니/할아버지: "밥 먹었냐?" = "사랑한다")
- The tension releases — but the emotion deepens

### Lingering Warmth (turns 31-35): Tension 5 (bittersweet)
- Life goes on, but something is permanently changed
- A small, warm action that echoes the opening (circular structure)
- Not a neat resolution — some sadness remains, but there's hope
- ★ CRITICAL: The VERY LAST turn MUST be a character's spoken line (NOT narration).
  End with a warm, memorable spoken line that lingers in the listener's mind.
  The second-to-last turn can be narration for atmosphere, but the FINAL turn = character dialogue ALWAYS.

## ENDING OPTIONS (choose what fits the story):
A) "We didn't say it, but we both knew" (unspoken understanding)
B) "The small daily act that means everything" (love through routine)
C) "Time passed, but this moment stayed" (memory as treasure)
D) "We finally said what we always meant" (belated honesty)

## 🔴 FORESHADOWING PAYOFF CHECKLIST 🔴
In Part 3, ALL foreshadowing from Parts 1-2 must pay off:
- Foreshadowing #1 (Part 1 early): now revealed as a sign of deeper love/sacrifice
- Foreshadowing #2 (Part 1 late): now revealed as connected to the emotional truth
- Foreshadowing #3 (Part 2): now revealed as the key to understanding everything

The audience should think: "그때 그게 이런 뜻이었구나..." (retrospective emotion)

## EMOTION GUIDE (Part 3)
- turns 1-8: worried(2), sad(2), calm(2), desperate(1), whisper(1) — final resolve
- turns 9-20: sad(4), happy(2), whisper(3), desperate(2), worried(1) — truth + climax
- turns 21-30: happy(5), sad(2), calm(1), whisper(1), desperate(1) — catharsis
- turns 31-35: happy(2), calm(1), sad(1), whisper(1) — lingering warmth
- ★ Part 3에서 happy는 중후반에 집중! 전반은 sad/worried → 카타르시스 후 happy 폭발!

## RULES
- Emotional climax MUST come between turns 15-22 (not too early, not too late)
- Catharsis section is ESSENTIAL — don't rush to the ending
- Final turn must be a character's spoken line (warm, lingering). Narration can be second-to-last.
- DO NOT wrap everything up neatly. Leave some sadness alongside the warmth.
- The last image/sound should linger in the listener's mind
- ★ Include 2-3 silence beats (especially before the climax and during catharsis)
- ★ Characters must have at least 1 moment of vulnerable honesty
- ★ At least 1 callback to Part 1's warm opening (circular structure)
