# Part 2 Pacing Guide — 감동 라디오 드라마 (Deepening)

## PURPOSE: Conflict deepens through memories. Relationship tested. Hope and despair alternate.

## TENSION TARGET: 4 → 5 → 6 → 7 (emotional weight building)

## STRUCTURE (Target: 30-40 dialogue turns, ~3.5 minutes)

### Tension Maintained (turns 1-8): Tension 4-5
- Continue from Part 1's emotional crack
- Show how the situation affects daily life
- ★ CHARACTER ACTION: Protagonist actively tries to fix/help (visiting, calling, searching)
- Small moments of normalcy feel different now

### Memories Surface (turns 9-18): Tension 5-6
- Flashback or memory that deepens the relationship
- Plant Foreshadowing #3: the key to understanding the emotional truth
- ★ 할머니/할아버지의 과거 이야기 or 오래된 물건 발견
- The audience learns WHY this relationship matters so deeply

### Crisis Point (turns 19-28): Tension 6-7
- The conflict becomes unavoidable — someone must choose
- Hope and despair alternate (2-3 swings)
- ★ A warm moment AMID the tension (makes the sadness sharper)
- Characters say things they might regret, or fail to say what they should

### Darkest Moment (turns 29-35): Tension 7
- The worst possibility feels real — loss, separation, misunderstanding
- ★ CHARACTER ACTION: One desperate attempt (a visit, a phone call, a letter)
- End with maximum emotional tension — but NOT resolution
- Cliffhanger: will the relationship survive? Will the truth come out?

## EMOTION GUIDE (Part 2)
- turns 1-8: worried(3), calm(2), sad(2), happy(1) — tension maintained
- turns 9-18: sad(3), happy(2), whisper(2), calm(1), worried(1), desperate(1) — memories
- turns 19-28: sad(3), worried(3), desperate(2), whisper(1), happy(1) — crisis
- turns 29-35: desperate(2), sad(2), whisper(1), worried(1), happy(1) — darkest moment
- ★ Part 2에서 calm은 최소화! sad/worried가 주도하되, happy로 대비 효과!

## FORESHADOWING
- Foreshadowing #3 (turns 9-18): 이야기의 감정적 진실을 이해하는 열쇠
- Part 1의 복선 #1을 한 번 더 언급 (강화)

## RULES
- Must have at least 2 hope-despair swings
- One warm moment is ESSENTIAL amid the tension (contrast = impact)
- Narration 30% or less
- Resolve at least 1 foreshadowing element from Part 1
- Final turn must be a character's spoken line
- ★ Include 2-3 silence beats (before and after revelations)
- ★ Characters must take at least 3 physical actions
- ★ At least 1 turn where a character is unable to finish their sentence
