# Part 1 Pacing Guide — 감동 라디오 드라마 (Setup)

## PURPOSE: Warm daily life. Plant emotional seeds. End with first crack in the warmth.

## TENSION TARGET: 2 → 3 → 4 → 5 (gentle unease emerging)

## STRUCTURE (Target: 30-40 dialogue turns, ~3.5 minutes)

### Warm Daily Life (turns 1-8): Tension 2
- Introduce protagonist through daily routine (cooking, walking, waiting)
- Show relationships through small actions, not exposition
- ★ CHARACTER ACTION: Doing something physical (cooking, cleaning, opening door)
- Establish the "normal" that will later be disrupted

### Relationships Revealed (turns 9-18): Tension 3
- Show how characters interact — love expressed through actions
- Plant Foreshadowing #1: a small detail that will gain meaning later
- ★ A warm moment that the audience will remember when it's threatened
- 할머니/할아버지: show love through food, gestures, not words

### First Unease (turns 19-28): Tension 4
- Something is slightly off — a missed call, an empty chair, a forgotten name
- Plant Foreshadowing #2: connected to the emotional core
- ★ CHARACTER ACTION: Protagonist tries to address what's wrong (calls, visits, searches)
- The audience should feel the sadness before the character does (dramatic irony)

### First Emotional Crack (turns 29-35): Tension 5
- A moment that reveals the real stakes — what could be lost
- The warmth from earlier now feels fragile
- ★ End with a line that lingers: an unfinished sentence, a quiet realization
- Cliffhanger: not dramatic shock, but emotional weight

## EMOTION GUIDE (Part 1)
- turns 1-8: calm(4), happy(3), whisper(1) — peaceful opening
- turns 9-18: calm(2), happy(3), worried(2), sad(1), whisper(1), desperate(1) — relationships + seeds
- turns 19-28: sad(3), worried(3), whisper(2), calm(1), happy(1) — unease growing
- turns 29-35: sad(3), desperate(1), whisper(1), worried(1), happy(1) — first crack
- ★ Part 1은 calm/happy가 앞부분에 집중! 뒤로 갈수록 sad/worried 비중 증가!

## FORESHADOWING CHECKLIST (Part 1)
- Foreshadowing #1 (turns 9-18): 나중에 감정적 의미가 드러날 사소한 디테일
- Foreshadowing #2 (turns 19-28): 이야기 핵심 갈등과 연결될 요소

## RULES
- Opening 8 turns must establish warmth (no conflict yet)
- At least 2 foreshadowing elements planted
- Narration 30% or less
- Final turn must be a character's spoken line
- ★ Include 2-3 silence beats (before emotional moments)
- ★ Characters must take at least 3 physical actions
