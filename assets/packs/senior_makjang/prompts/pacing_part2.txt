# Part 2 Pacing Guide — 막장 드라마 (Crisis)

## PURPOSE: Secrets unravel. Alliances shift. Everyone cornered. Maximum chaos.

## TENSION TARGET: 5 → 7 → 9 → 8 (escalation → peak confrontation → aftermath)

## STRUCTURE (Target: 30-40 dialogue turns, ~3.5 minutes)

### Aftermath & Regrouping (turns 1-8): Tension 5-6
- Continue from Part 1's explosion — characters deal with the fallout
- New alliances form, old ones crack
- ★ CHARACTER ACTION: Secret phone call, hidden meeting, confronting an ally
- Someone is planning revenge or damage control

### Escalation (turns 9-18): Tension 7-8
- New information surfaces — worse than Part 1's revelation
- Plant Foreshadowing #3: the key to the final twist
- ★ A 2차 반전: what seemed like the truth was only half of it
- Trust collapses — "Even YOU were lying?"

### Peak Confrontation (turns 19-28): Tension 8-9
- The biggest clash — two or more characters face off
- ★ All masks off — raw, ugly truth
- Hope-despair swing: a brief moment where reconciliation seems possible → crushed
- ★ CHARACTER ACTION: Evidence revealed (letter, photo, document), door slammed, someone leaves

### The Wreckage (turns 29-35): Tension 7-8
- The damage is done — the family/relationship is fractured
- ★ One devastating quiet moment after all the screaming
- Someone is left alone — the silence is louder than the shouting
- Cliffhanger: the worst is yet to come (a new secret, a consequence)

## EMOTION GUIDE (Part 2)
- turns 1-8: worried(3), angry(2), calm(1), whisper(1), sad(1) — aftermath
- turns 9-18: angry(4), desperate(2), whisper(2), worried(1), sad(1) — escalation
- turns 19-28: angry(4), desperate(3), sad(1), whisper(1), worried(1) — peak clash
- turns 29-35: sad(3), desperate(1), whisper(2), calm(1) — wreckage
- ★ Part 2는 angry+desperate가 60% 이상! calm은 wreckage에서만 잠깐!

## FORESHADOWING
- Foreshadowing #3 (turns 9-18): 최종 반전을 이해하는 결정적 단서
- Part 1의 복선 #1: 새로운 맥락에서 재해석

## RULES
- Must have at least 1 major 반전 (reversal)
- Must have 1 hope-despair swing (brief hope → crushed)
- Narration 30% or less
- Resolve at least 1 foreshadowing from Part 1
- Final turn must be a character's spoken line
- ★ Include 3-5 silence beats (after each major revelation)
- ★ Characters must take at least 4 physical actions
- ★ At least 2 turns where characters speak in 1-word or 1-sentence bursts
- ★ At least 1 cold, icy calm line amid the chaos ("나 다 알아.")
