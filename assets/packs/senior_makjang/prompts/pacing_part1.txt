# Part 1 Pacing Guide — 막장 드라마 (Setup)

## PURPOSE: Surface peace shattered. Secrets planted. End with first explosion.

## TENSION TARGET: 3 → 4 → 6 → 7 (calm surface → first cracks → first eruption)

## STRUCTURE (Target: 30-40 dialogue turns, ~3.5 minutes)

### Surface Peace (turns 1-7): Tension 3
- Family gathering, meal, or celebration — everyone seems fine
- But subtle signs: forced smiles, averted eyes, loaded silences
- ★ CHARACTER ACTION: Arriving, sitting down, pouring tea — surface normalcy
- Establish relationships and power dynamics through small interactions

### Seeds of Conflict (turns 8-16): Tension 4-5
- A careless comment, a misplaced item, a phone that rings at the wrong time
- Plant Foreshadowing #1: a detail that will become explosive later
- ★ Someone says something with double meaning — audience catches it, characters don't
- Power plays: who controls the conversation? Who stays silent?

### Cracks Appear (turns 17-26): Tension 5-6
- The mask slips — someone's composure breaks momentarily
- Plant Foreshadowing #2: connected to the central secret
- ★ CHARACTER ACTION: Confronting someone privately, finding something hidden, making a suspicious call
- Suspicion builds — "Something is wrong with this family"

### First Explosion (turns 27-35): Tension 7
- Someone says what shouldn't be said — or reveals what should stay hidden
- ★ The "가면이 벗겨지는 순간" — one devastating line
- An argument, a walkout, a slap of truth
- Cliffhanger: a bombshell that changes everything

## EMOTION GUIDE (Part 1)
- turns 1-7: calm(3), worried(2), happy(1), whisper(1) — surface peace
- turns 8-16: worried(3), calm(2), whisper(2), angry(1), sad(1) — seeds planted
- turns 17-26: angry(3), worried(2), whisper(2), desperate(1), sad(1), calm(1) — cracks
- turns 27-35: angry(4), desperate(2), sad(1), whisper(1), worried(1) — explosion
- ★ Part 1 앞부분은 calm/worried, 뒷부분은 angry/desperate로 급격 전환!

## FORESHADOWING CHECKLIST (Part 1)
- Foreshadowing #1 (turns 8-16): 나중에 폭발할 비밀의 실마리
- Foreshadowing #2 (turns 17-26): 중심 갈등과 직결되는 증거/단서

## RULES
- Opening 7 turns: surface peace ONLY (no conflict yet — the calm before storm)
- At least 2 foreshadowing elements planted
- Narration 30% or less
- Final turn must be a character's spoken line (impactful)
- ★ Include 3-5 silence beats (especially after shocking lines)
- ★ Characters must take at least 4 physical actions
- ★ At least 1 turn where 존대→반말 전환 occurs
