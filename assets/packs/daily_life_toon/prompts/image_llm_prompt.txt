For each scene, produce a layered video-toon visual brief:
1. background_location: reusable location name
2. background_prompt: no people, no text, no UI, clean center space
3. actor_id: one of actor_adult_woman_01, sunja_grandma, deokbae_grandpa, misuk_auntie, jinwoo_young_man, nari_young_woman
4. role_id: story role for this episode (the same actor_id can play different roles in another episode)
5. expression: neutral, talking, worried, angry, sad, happy
6. pose: standing, seated, leaning, listening, looking_away
7. camera: medium shot, close-up, wide shot
8. layer_grounding: describe where the half-body character stands or sits in the background
Use character_id only as a legacy alias when a downstream tool has not migrated to actor_id yet.
Avoid floating cards, bank UI, phone UI, and explanatory overlays unless the script explicitly requires a physical prop.
Prefer waist-up or half-body character staging over neck-only busts.
Use the exact same background_location for continuous scenes, and change it only when the story actually moves.
