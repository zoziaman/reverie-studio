# Part 2 Pacing Guide — Horror Radio Drama (Escalation)

## PURPOSE: Horror intensifies. Each scene scarier than the last. Protagonist trapped.

## TENSION TARGET: 4 → 5 → 7 → 8

## STRUCTURE (Target: 30-40 dialogue turns, ~3.5 minutes)

### Aftermath (turns 1-5): Tension 4
- Brief recap through character REACTION (NOT narrator exposition)
- Protagonist tries to rationalize or seek help
- But something has fundamentally changed
- ★ Protagonist ACTION: Calls someone, goes somewhere for help, tries to leave

### Investigation (turns 6-15): Tension 5
- Protagonist ACTIVELY tries to understand what's happening
- Each discovery makes it WORSE, not better
- A trusted person doesn't believe them OR is part of the problem
- ★ FORESHADOWING #3: The detail from Part 1 appears in a disturbing new context
- ★ Protagonist ACTION: Searches for evidence, confronts someone, tries a plan

### Trap (turns 16-28): Tension 7
- Protagonist realizes they can't escape/ignore this
- The horror becomes personal (it knows them, targets them specifically)
- Rules of the horror become clearer but more terrifying
- Environment turns hostile (familiar places become threatening)
- Include 2-3 silence beats
- ★ Protagonist ACTION: Desperate measures (barricading, fighting back, destroying something)

### "It's Real" Moment (turns 29-40): Tension 8
- Undeniable confrontation with the horror
- Protagonist's worst fear confirmed
- All escape routes closed
- End on maximum dread — the listener feels trapped too

## 🔴 MANDATORY: FALSE HOPE MOMENT (turns 12-18) 🔴
You MUST include ONE false hope moment in Part 2:
1. Something seems to improve (the sound stops, a friend arrives, morning comes)
2. Protagonist and audience briefly relax
3. Then it gets WORSE than before — the hope was a trap
4. This emotional whiplash is essential for horror pacing

Example:
- Turn 14: Friend arrives: "야, 나 왔어. 괜찮아?"
- Turn 15: Protagonist relaxes: "다행이다... 너 와줘서 정말..."
- Turn 16: Friend says something impossible: "근데... 나 여기 오는 길에 너 봤는데?"
- Turn 17: [Silence beat: "..."]

## EMOTION GUIDE (Part 2)
- turns 1-5: worried(2), calm(1), scared(1), whisper(1) — aftermath
- turns 6-15: scared(3), worried(3), whisper(2), desperate(1), calm(1) — investigation
- turns 16-28: scared(4), desperate(3), whisper(2), angry(2), worried(2) — trap
- turns 29-40: desperate(3), scared(3), angry(2), whisper(2), worried(1), sad(1) — "it's real"
- ★ Part 2에서 calm은 1-3턴만! 대부분 worried/scared/desperate 사용!

## RULES
- Part 2 is FASTER than Part 1. Shorter scenes, quicker cuts.
- Each scene must be scarier than the previous
- The "trap" revelation should feel inevitable in hindsight
- No new major characters (deepen existing ones)
- ★ DO NOT reveal the twist yet! Save it for Part 3 turns 20-30!
- ★ Protagonist must take at least 4 physical actions
- ★ Include 3-4 silence beats
